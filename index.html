Stress-Free, Sensory-Friendly Clothing for Kids with Autism

No tags. No wrong way. Just soft, reversible tees and hoodies designed for comfort and independence.

Our Story

As a parent of a child with autism, I know the daily struggle: seams, tags, scratchy fabric, and the constant battle of inside-out or backwards clothes. That’s why I created InsideOut Clothing — a reversible, sensory-friendly line made to reduce stress and give kids confidence in dressing themselves.

Smart Value for Parents

Our reversible tees and hoodies cost a little more upfront, but the math makes sense:

  • Basic tee ($12): ~33¢ per wear
  • InsideOut reversible tee ($24): ~67¢ per wear (twice the use, fewer battles)
  • Reversible hoodie ($60): under 50¢ per wear

More comfort. Fewer meltdowns. True value.
